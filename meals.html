Dexamphetamine eating strategy

Your dex suppresses appetite from roughly 30-60 min after taking it until mid-afternoon. The strategy is simple: front-load calories before the drug kicks in, use liquid meals when you have zero appetite, and capitalise on your appetite returning in the evening. The pre-dex breakfast and the evening meals are your two most important calorie windows. Never skip the morning meal.

Daily supplement schedule

Creatine is updated to 10g per day based on your body mass — split into two 5g doses for best absorption. At 100kg+ this dose is well supported by current research. Dex and creatine both increase water demand — 3 to 4 litres minimum daily. All over-the-counter supplements only. Prescribed medications (testosterone, dexamphetamine) are managed through your doctor — not listed here.

Weekly grocery list

This list covers a full week of eating to target. Buy in bulk where possible — chicken breast, eggs, oats, rice, and protein powder are your weekly staples. Estimated weekly cost: $180-220 including supplements.

Sunday meal prep — 60 minutes

One Sunday prep session sets up most of your weekday eating. The goal is that on any given morning or evening you can assemble a full meal in under 5 minutes. Everything listed is designed around that principle.
